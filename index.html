Special Penalty Enforcements

  1. Roughing the Passer – The penalty for roughing the passer on a completed forward pass will be enforced from the dead ball spot when the run ends beyond Team A's scrimmage line and no change of possession has occurred. Tack on the yardage to the end of the run.

  2. Change of Possession – If both teams foul during a down where there is a change of team possession, the team last in possession may keep the ball if:

    1. They did not foul prior to the change of possession.
    2. They decline all live ball fouls by the opponent prior to the final change of possession.
